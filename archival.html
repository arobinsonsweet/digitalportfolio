Archival

We Demand: Artifacts of Activism from The New School Archives

In 2020, I curated this temporary exhibit of archival objects at The New School, where I worked as an archivist. The display featured items from The New School Archives’ collections documenting over sixty years of campus protest.

https://histories.newschool.edu/node/416

Oral Histories

As an archivist at The New School Archives & Special Collections, I initiated oral history projects focused on documenting student activism at the university. I conducted over ten in-depth interviews with alumni who had participated in anti-Vietnam War protests as well as activism around diversity, divestment, and equity on campus. I also processed the interviews and created finding aids for these collections.

https://findingaids.archives.newschool.edu/agents/people/902
